About Us

About South Gujarat Group (SGG)

A community built on shared roots, mutual trust, and the spirit of giving back.

🌟 SGG Vision

The South Gujarat Group community is for folks with ethnic roots in South Gujarat, currently residing in North America (USA & Canada). The vision is to form a virtual group supplemented by regional get-togethers with the following purpose:

🤝 Assist & Guide Newcomers

  • Provide a helping hand to students and families coming from India for studies, jobs, or business.
  • Resume review, mentoring, LinkedIn networking, job/internship referrals and professional 1:1 introductions.
ℹ️ This group will not provide monetary assistance for accommodation, tuition, etc.

📡 Keep in Touch

North America is quite diverse and geographically spread out — keeping in touch will prove to be mutually beneficial for social and professional networking across the US and Canada.

🎓

Student Support

How We Help Each Other

Four pillars of support that every newcomer and professional can count on.

💼

Professional Guidance

Resume reviews, mock interviews, job referrals, salary advice, LinkedIn profile tips, and 1:1 mentorship from experienced SGG professionals.

🛂

Immigration Discussions

Real peer experiences on F1, CPT, OPT, H1B, Green Card, and STEM OPT. Navigate visa processes with guidance from people who've done it.

🌱

Newcomer Support

From pre-departure checklists to setting up a bank account, SSN, SIM card, and finding accommodation — SGG members guide you before and after arrival.

📍

Regional Networking

Connect with SGG members in your metro area — Bay Area, Edison NJ, Toronto, Dallas, Chicago, and beyond. Local meetups and support circles.
